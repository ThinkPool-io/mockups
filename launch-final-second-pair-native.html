Questions before the pair starts

What Thinkpool does, and what it does not.

Is Thinkpool a coding agent?

No. Thinkpool is the shared room around the Claude and Codex lanes the pair already uses.

Can both people steer the lanes?

Both people can enter and steer the live lanes exposed in the room while the owner’s bridge is online.

Does the host have to stay online?

Yes, for live agent work. The agents and repository remain on the owner’s host.

Do lanes share all context automatically?

No. Peer provides bounded recent activity. Dispatch opens a fresh Claude or Codex lane or hands a task to an existing live agent lane under the room’s permission gates. The pair remains responsible for the plan and review.
