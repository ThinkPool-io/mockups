Construction

10px-radius signal disc. The 2.65px Wellspring arc and state glyph are carved through in terminal-black.

Why it fits

The circle echoes water, ripples, and status dots. It removes the miniature app-tile feeling while preserving maximum painted area.

Watch-out

At a glance it can resemble a presence dot. The carved state mark must always remain visible; never render the disc by itself.
