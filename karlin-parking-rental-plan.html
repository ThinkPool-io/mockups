The drive path

Eight gates to rent

Work top to bottom. Checkboxes save on this device. Do not skip ahead: every later protection depends on authority and access working first.

1

Prove the right to sublet

Before listing

Use the signed final lease—not the blank draft—and remove the contradiction with a one-paragraph landlord consent.

Why this is non-negotiable: clause 6.9 looks permissive when the tenant lives there, but Article 8 separately names unapproved subletting as a termination ground. Parking is also not clearly “part of the apartment” under the residential exception.
2

Test the access loop

20 minutes

The tenant must be able to enter, park, leave on foot, return and retrieve the car using only the access items you intend to provide.

Record the route and any height, width or turning constraints. If pedestrians must use the vehicle ramp, verify that the building rules allow it before advertising.
3

Build the evidence pack

30 minutes

Good photos attract the right car; measured dimensions stop bad viewings and arguments.

4

Set terms before leads arrive

15 minutes

Make one offer and keep it consistent. Recommended: 3,490 Kč monthly, paid in advance, long-term use, one registered vehicle.

The sublease must automatically end earlier if your main lease, landlord consent or building access right ends.
5

Publish narrowly

Day 1–14

Start where direct local demand lives. Use the prepared Czech copy; keep the exact bay number and access details out of the public listing.

6

Screen the driver

10 minutes / lead

Select for reliability and vehicle fit, not the fastest “I’ll take it.” Verify identity in person and collect only what the contract needs.

7

Sign, pay, then hand over

One meeting

No remote leaves your hand until the agreement is signed and cleared funds cover the first rent and deposits.

8

Operate and close cleanly

Monthly + exit

Keep a tiny ledger, act on late rent immediately, and make the exit as documented as the handover.

Never improvise self-help towing, wheel-clamping or blocking the vehicle. For unauthorized use, document it and follow the building manager’s and legal process.

04 · Agreement skeleton

Clauses that protect you

The final document should be a parking-space sublease, because you are the tenant rather than the owner.

  1. Exact space, level, address and permitted access route
  2. Named subtenant plus one make/model/registration plate
  3. Fixed term ending no later than 30 June 2027
  4. Automatic earlier end with the main lease, consent or access right
  5. Rent, due date, deposits and documented deductions
  6. Express termination reasons and notice period
  7. No storage, repairs, charging, hazardous materials or further sharing
  8. Compliance with fire, safety, garage and house rules
  9. Responsibility for damage, guests, lost devices and access charges
  10. Return of devices, inspection and deposit settlement at exit
  11. No right to the apartment, cellar or residential areas
  12. Czech law; Czech text controls if the agreement is bilingual
The clean finish

Keep it boring. Keep it paid.

Every month

One bank transfer, one ledger line, one quick check that the main lease and landlord consent remain valid.

If rent is late

Send the written notice required by the agreement immediately. Do not let informal extensions become the operating model.

If access is lost

Notify the driver, landlord and building manager; use the contract’s early-end mechanism and document every replacement cost.

At tax time

Keep rent and expense records. Czech Financial Administration treats rental income as taxable income in the relevant category; confirm your personal filing position with an accountant.
